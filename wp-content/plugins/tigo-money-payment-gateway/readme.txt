Tigo Money Payment Gateway allows Tigo Money Partners to connect to our services and WooCommerce.

== Description ==
Tigo Money Payment Gateway is an application that allows the interconnection between Tigo Money and an online commerce. This will allow more than one million customers that already use Tigo Money as a payment method to make online transactions from their cell phones. Transactions will be debited from the customers Tigo Money wallet and transferred to the commerce's bank account in a period of 24 hours. 


**Example**
When you are in the checkout:

1. Select as payment method, Tigo Money
1. Enter your MSISDN
1. you will be redirected to the payment gateway
1. Enter your MSISDN
1. You will receive a OTP by SMS
1. Enter OTP
1. Enter your Tigo Money pin
1. Confirms the payment
1. You will be redirected back to your store

In order to enroll click on the following link 
[http://www.tigomoney.com.sv/content/pygw_afiliacion](http://www.tigomoney.com.sv/content/pygw_afiliacion)

== Installation ==
1. Upload the plugin files to the `/wp-content/plugins/tigo-money-payment-gateway` directory, or install the plugin through the WordPress plugins screen directly.
2. Activate the plugin through the \'Plugins\' screen in WordPress
3. Use the WooCoomerce->Settings->Checkout Tab->Tigo Money Payment Gateway to configure the plugin
4. Contact Tigo Money to get the Tigo Merchant Account, Merchant PIN Number, Consumer Key, Consumer Secret.
5. You can check Debug Log and sandbox to check with Tigo Money if all transaction are doing it okay.
